About IntPub

What We're Building

IntPub is a tool for researchers, academics, and scientists to self-publish their works on uncensorable & decentralized networks. The protocol is a a scholarship-oriented bridge between existing technology.

Our Mission

IntPub's mission is to make scientific publishing accessible to all, and to make scientific knowledge free to access. We strongly believe that the rights to explore, research and publish are fundamental.

Our Vision

We envision a future where the pipeline of science is transparent from idea through to publication. We believe establishing IntPub as the base infrastructure layer for science will make this possible.
